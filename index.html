Why Choose Sopaana?

Sopaana Face Mouth Care Clinic is a modern healthcare facility that combines traditional wisdom with contemporary medical practices. We specialize in three core areas:

  • General Physician: Complete healthcare solutions for you and your family

Our team of experienced professionals is committed to providing personalized care in a comfortable, welcoming environment.
